Method and trust notes

Conversion tools use standard formulas and live rates where available, but results should still be verified for critical use cases.

  • Currency rates can change rapidly after page load.
  • Rounded outputs may differ from institutional systems.
  • Use these tools for planning, not contractual settlement.
